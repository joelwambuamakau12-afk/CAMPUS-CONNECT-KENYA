About

Built for the walk between lecture halls and job offers

Every year, thousands of Kenyan graduates leave campus with a degree and zero plan for what comes next. We're the plan — a single, honest place to find real openings from real companies. No middlemen, no fake agents, no "send us KES 500 processing fee."

Mission

Close the gap between classroom and career

We give every student — from Kisumu to Kakamega, Mombasa to Meru — equal access to the internships, attachments and jobs that used to depend on who you knew.

Vision

A Kenya where no talent is wasted

A country where a first-class degree from any university, public or private, opens the same doors — because the opportunity board is the same for everyone.

Goals

10,000 placements by 2027

We're building toward ten thousand verified placements — internships, attachments and full-time roles — tracked from application to offer letter.

Why we exist

Because "who do you know" shouldn't decide your career

CampusConnect started as a WhatsApp group sharing internship links between three friends at different universities. It's now a platform built for every campus.
